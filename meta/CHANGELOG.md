## Version 0.2

### General
- Removed references to the Politecnico di Milano throughout the document, to make it more generic.
- Created a new cover and back cover themed after the CIDS.
- Removed Appendix A. Appendix B is now Appendix A, and so on.
- Several typos were fixed, and some pharases were modified for clarity.
- Removed the note about the storage solutions provided by the Politecnico di Milano, and added a generic section instead. This means that section 7.1 is larger.
- Added a table (`tab:dosanddonts`) with a list of what to include in the DMP and what not to include.
